About Me

Hello, I'm Nahiyan, a Textile Designer from Bangladesh.

I graduated with a Bachelor's degree in Textile Design from Academy of Art University in San Francisco. Through my education and professional experience, I specialize in print development and fashion design, creating innovative, market-driven prints using NED Graphics and Adobe Creative Suite. With over seven years of experience, I have a strong grasp of textile materials, color theory, and trend forecasting, ensuring designs that are both striking and commercially successful.

While creativity drives my work, I also excel at fast decision-making, leadership, and problem-solving. From refining colorways to seamless digital-to-fabric transitions, I approach each project with both artistry and technical precision. My goal is to craft impactful designs that merge aesthetics with functionality.
